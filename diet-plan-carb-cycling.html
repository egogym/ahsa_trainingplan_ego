Supplements & Hydration

Daily Supplements

  • • Whey Protein: 2 servings daily
  • • Creatine: 5g daily (post-workout)
  • • Optional: Omega-3s, Multivitamins

Hydration Guidelines

  • • Water Intake: 2.5–3 liters daily
  • • Monitor urine color (pale yellow ideal)
  • • Increase water during workouts
  • • Limit caffeine after 4 PM

Key Nutritional Strategies

Meal Preparation Guidelines

  • • Prep meals in advance for 3-4 days
  • • Invest in a reliable food scale for precise portioning
  • • Use airtight containers for meal storage
  • • Keep protein sources and vegetables readily available

Performance Optimization

  • • Maintain consistent sleep (7-8 hours nightly)
  • • Refeed meal every two weeks to replenish glycogen
  • • Adjust portions based on progress and body response
  • • Stay consistent with meal timing and macronutrients

Understanding Carb Cycling

Carb cycling is a strategic dietary approach that alternates between high and low carbohydrate intake. This method optimizes metabolism, supports muscle growth, and enhances fat loss by manipulating insulin sensitivity and metabolic flexibility.

Normal-Carb Days

Provide energy for intense training, support muscle glycogen replenishment, and boost metabolism.

Zero-Carb Days

Promote fat burning, improve insulin sensitivity, and allow metabolic reset between high-carb periods.

Key Benefits

Enhanced body composition, improved nutrient partitioning, and sustained metabolic efficiency.
