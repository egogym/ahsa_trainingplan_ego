Pro Split Training Plan

5-Day Training Program with Carb-Cycling Diet Focus

Program Overview

This comprehensive 5-day training plan follows a Pro Split structure, tailored for carb-cycling diet and achieving optimal body composition. Each day targets specific muscle groups, incorporating both cardio and flexibility work for complete athletic development.

Progression Guidelines

Training Tips

  • Increase weight gradually by 2-5% weekly
  • Focus on perfect form to avoid injuries
  • Adjust cardio intensity based on energy levels
  • Use carb intake strategically on training days

Recovery Tips

  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep per night
  • Stay hydrated (2-3 liters daily)
  • Maintain proper nutrition
  • Listen to your body's signals
